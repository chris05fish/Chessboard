  • King: can only move one space, up, down or sideways
  • Queen: can move diagonally, up and down, and side to side
  • Bishop: can only move diagonally
  • Knight: can move ether two spaces up/down and one over, or two spaces over and one up/down
  • Castle: can ether go up or down
  • Pawn: can only move forward one space but attacks one diagonally, if it is the first pawn move then you can move two spaces up
